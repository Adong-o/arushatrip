Explore Arusha on a Budget

Budget Breakdown

  • Accommodation: KSh 4,000 for 2-3 nights in budget lodgings
  • Food: KSh 2,000 for affordable local meals
  • Transportation: KSh 2,000 for local transport
  • Activities and Sightseeing: KSh 5,000 for budget activities and entrance fees

Affordable Activities

Explore Arusha City

  • Clock Tower: A quick and free landmark visit.
  • Central Market: Experience local culture and buy affordable snacks and fresh produce.

Visit Local Markets

  • Maasai Market: Perfect for buying souvenirs and experiencing local crafts.

Nature Walks and Free Attractions

  • Themi Falls Leisure Park: Enjoy a scenic walk and picnic at a minimal cost.
  • Lake Duluti: Entry fees are low, and you can enjoy a peaceful walk around the crater lake.

Cultural Experiences

  • Mto wa Mbu Village: Opt for a self-guided tour.
  • Tanzanite Experience: Learn about this unique gemstone with free museum entry.

Arusha Declaration Museum

  • Cost: Very minimal entrance fee.

Accommodation Suggestions

  • Greenland Arusha Hostel: Prices can be as low as KSh 1,500 per night.
  • Guesthouses like "White House of Tanzania": Prices around KSh 2,000 per night.

Food Recommendations

  • Local Eateries: Meals at places like "Khan’s BBQ" can cost around KSh 200-300 per meal.
  • Street Food: Affordable options like “chipsi mayai” for about KSh 100-200 per serving.

Transportation Tips

  • Dala-Dalas: Local minibuses are a cheap option, costing around KSh 50-100 per ride.
  • Boda-Bodas: Motorbike taxis for short distances typically cost about KSh 50-150.
  • Shared Taxis: Useful for longer distances, costs vary depending on the distance.

Sample Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

  • Morning: Arrive in Arusha and check into your accommodation.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Central Market and Clock Tower.
  • Evening: Dinner at a local eatery like Khan’s BBQ.

Day 2: Nature and Culture

  • Morning: Visit Lake Duluti for a walk.
  • Afternoon: Explore Themi Falls Leisure Park.
  • Evening: Stroll around Arusha and try street food for dinner.

Day 3: Cultural Experience and Departure

  • Morning: Visit the Maasai Market for souvenirs.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Arusha Declaration Museum.
  • Evening: Prepare for departure and enjoy a local dinner.

Additional Tips

  • Exchange Rate: Ensure you exchange some currency to Tanzanian Shillings (TZS) for convenience.
  • Bargain: Always negotiate for better prices in markets and when booking tours.
  • Safety: Keep an eye on your belongings and avoid isolated areas at night.
